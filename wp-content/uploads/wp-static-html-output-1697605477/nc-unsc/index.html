Letter from the chairperson

Greetings Delegates,

I am Dhruv Chaudhary, and it is my honor to serve as the chairperson of the United Nations Security Council at the Shishukunj North Campus MUN Conference 2023.I am delighted to welcome you all to this committee on behalf of the entire executive bureau.

I am a tenth-grader who just wants to leave a mark on this world. I am a bit of a workaholic and am mostly engaged in some activity or another. When I am not buried under a heap of work, I can be seen vibing to Polyphia or Call Me Karizma with my guitar. I am also a voracious reader who enjoys mystery novels. Along with this, I am also a football enthusiast and an ardent supporter of the Mercedes AMG-Petronas Formula One Team.

I attended my first MUN in the year 2021. As a first-time delegate, I too was a bundle of nerves and had absolutely no will to speak up in front of my seniors. However, it was the supportive bureau that brought me out of my comfort zone and helped me blossom into a passionate public speaker, thus bringing me to this platform, where I stand as your chairperson.

MUNs have played a major role in my personality development. I have instilled a sense of diplomacy, confidence, cooperation, and discipline in my life. I truly hope to do justice to my position as chairperson of this committee and ensure that all of you have an enriching MUN experience.

I can assure you that this session of the Security Council will be full of surprises and will keep you on your toes. The committee is a crisis committee, so it will require all delegates to work even harder than a regular GA or ECOSOC committee. Immense research, knowledge of the agenda, and quick and analytical thinking will pave the way to your success.

If there is one thing I can guarantee you, it is that no doubts or questions of yours will go unanswered, however silly they may seem. Feel free to reach out to me in case of any queries regarding the ROP or the agenda, or if you simply hate Red Bull too.

Prepare well and buckle up for an exhilarating MUN filled with fiery debates and discussions. I look forward to meeting all of you.

Warm Regards

Dhruv Chaudhary

Chairperson

United Nations Security Council

Organ Description

The United Nations Security Council (UNSC) plays a crucial role in international affairs by promoting global peace and security. Composed of 15 member states, including five permanent members with veto power, it deliberates on pressing issues, authorizes military interventions, and imposes sanctions. The UNSC serves as a platform for diplomatic negotiations and conflict resolution, striving to prevent conflicts and maintain stability. Its decisions carry significant weight and influence in shaping the course of international relations and addressing threats to international peace.

Agenda : Addressing The South China Sea Conflict

The South China Sea has been a long-disputed area with various countries such as Vietnam, Thailand, Malaysia, Taiwan, Brunei and China establishing their EEZ over this area. It is an area of economic importance that lies in large reserves of oil, natural gas, and aquatic life among others and has trade routes amounting up to $5.3 trillion annually. Various nations hold conflicting claims over this region and follow various policies and commit various crimes to uphold their claims. The rising tensions can act as a tipping point for World War III.
